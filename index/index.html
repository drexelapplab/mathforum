PEx Color Sandbox v0.31

Hello, and welcome to the PEx Color Sandbox! Here, you can generate PermissionsEx commands that give prefixes and suffixes to players. The information you put in the fields below will simulate the effect of issuing the /pex [user] prefix and /pex [user] suffix commands, and you can see the changes in real-time!
